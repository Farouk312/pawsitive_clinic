About Us

A clinic built on passion for your pet’s health

Pawsitive Veterinary Clinic is the premier care destination for pets in Cairo. We believe every animal deserves complete medical care in a calm, luxurious setting that reflects our dedication and love.

We combine modern veterinary technology with a warm, compassionate approach for every case. Our team of top specialist vets is here to serve you and your furry family around the clock.
